Our Story

One summer, in the midst of a pandemic, around a year at the time of me writing this, a young boy named David had a dream, an idea, a breakthrough! He thought, since he had all this freetime because of the pandemic, why not try and learn how to code, should be fun, he said. And it was! This idea though had been broght to David from his best friend since he was 0 years old. He enjoyed it so much that he made a cool website for his fake company. A month later, that fake company wasn't that fake anymore. It had turned into a multi hour, multi dollar buisiness. David was finally happy after being a little bored for months on end. Now, David Industries is the pride and joy of David, and has brought him much pleasure, and, best of all, money!
